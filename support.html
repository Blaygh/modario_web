FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn’t the Discover page showing anything?

If your Discover page is empty, add more items to your wardrobe. Discover learns from your style and the clothing in your wardrobe, so uploading more relevant pieces helps improve recommendations over time.

How do I plan an outfit?

You can plan an outfit in two ways. First, tap an outfit and then tap Plan on the outfit detail page to choose a date. Second, go to the Calendar page, tap any date, and plan from your saved outfits for that day.

How does wardrobe upload work?

For now, Modario supports up to three image uploads at a time. After you submit your image or images, our servers process them in the background. You’ll then get a notification to review detected items. If you miss it, tap the button at the top right of the Wardrobe page to see completed uploads or uploads still waiting for review.

Why are wardrobe insights showing the wrong item count?

Wardrobe insights can take a few minutes to refresh after wardrobe changes. Give it a little time or refresh the app, and the correct item count should appear.
