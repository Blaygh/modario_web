About Modario

Modario is building a smarter way to decide what to wear.

Most people have full closets but still struggle to put outfits together every day. Modario turns your wardrobe into a digital system that helps you discover outfits, plan what to wear, and make faster decisions with the clothes you already own.

We’re starting with one simple goal: remove the daily friction of getting dressed.

Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you join the Modario waitlist, we collect your email address, which you provide voluntarily on our website.

At this stage, we do not collect additional personal data, such as payment information, addresses, or sensitive personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your email address is used solely to notify you when Modario launches, provide product updates, send early access invitations, and communicate important launch announcements.

We do not use your email for unrelated marketing, and we do not sell or rent your personal information.

3. No Sale of Personal Information

Your email address will never be sold, rented, or traded to third parties. We do not share your personal information with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing companies.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Your email may be stored using secure infrastructure or trusted service providers that help manage waitlist registrations and notifications.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services such as email notification platforms, hosting providers, and analytics services.

These providers may process your email only to help us operate the waitlist and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You may request removal from the waitlist at any time by contacting neku@modario.io.

7. Children's Privacy

The Modario waitlist is not directed toward individuals under the age of 13. If we become aware data was collected from a child without proper consent, we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted with a revised Last Updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, contact neku@modario.io.
